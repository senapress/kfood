    Welcome back to Chelsea's K-Food Recipes! Today, we’re making a beloved Korean summer classic — Paldo Bibimmyeon (팔도 비빔면). While it has a pleasant kick, it's far milder than Buldak Bokkeum Myeon (불닭볶음면), so it’s enjoyable even for those who don’t love extreme heat. And trust me — boiled egg and cucumber are a must.
